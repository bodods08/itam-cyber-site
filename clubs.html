Cybersecurity and Ethical Hacking Club

This club helps students improve their cybersecurity skills through hands-on learning and personal development. Members learn about network penetration testing, software protection, and cybersecurity ethics.

What the Club Does:

  • ✓ Practice cybersecurity skills
  • ✓ Participate in competitions
  • ✓ Attend workshops and guest talks
  • ✓ Work on real-world cybersecurity challenges
